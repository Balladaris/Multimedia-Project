The Main Idea


The main idea for this website is to give people a good idea of what Kansas City is about and known for. When it comes to BBQ, KC is king and we wanted to give people some of our favorite places to explore to. We also wanted to give unique ideas to check out when it comes to when they come to KC for an experience they would not be able to get in another city. I think if you need some ideas of where to go in KC, we have you covered and hope you enjoy.
